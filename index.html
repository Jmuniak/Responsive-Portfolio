About Me

I have experience in both technical and executive talent acquisiton, software sales, insurance sales, and I'm new to Full Stack Dev but enjoy it immensely(so far). My hobbies include; disc golfing, making cold brew, board sports, video games, wood working, grilling, comedy shows, science fiction. I have built a gaming PC and have a flight simulator set up at home so I can fly spaceships in Star Citizen when I have free time.
